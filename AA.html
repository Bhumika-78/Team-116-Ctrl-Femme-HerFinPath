Meet Esha Chawla

Meet Esha Chawla, a woman who has faced many challenges in life—family struggles, financial difficulties, and countless hurdles. But she never gave up. At the age of 42, with the support and encouragement of her daughters, she decided to build something of her own. That’s how Aarogyam Ayurveda was born—a brand that brings the goodness of homemade Ayurvedic care to everyone.

With her deep love for nature and traditional wisdom, Isha started making chemical-free Ayurvedic shampoo and ubtan face packs, crafted with pure ingredients. Her journey from struggle to success is an inspiration, proving that it’s never too late to follow your dreams. Today, she stands proud as a woman entrepreneur, bringing natural and authentic care straight from home to you.
